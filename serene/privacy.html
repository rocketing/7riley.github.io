Serene Privacy

Minimal. Calm. Intentional.

Serene only stores what is necessary to function.

Stored Data

  • Linked Last.fm username
  • Reminders you create (until triggered)

Not Stored

  • Message content
  • Direct messages (DMs)
  • Email Addresses
  • Personal data outside commands

Serene has disabled Last.fm cover art is intentionally disabled for safety.

Serene do not monitor your Last.fm account outside of /fm, /leaderboard, /toptracks, /topartists & /link lastfm.

Serene do not monitor/scrape your Discord profile for information.

Serene do not log anything (other than your Last.fm username)

Serene does not use user content to train/create AI models.

Serene does not sell/auction/give away any data it does save.

Serene has no moderation tracking nor analytics tracking.
